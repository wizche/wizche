--- layout: post title: TiSat1 (HB9DE) in ORBIT ! date: '2010-07-26T00:28:00.000-07:00' author: Wizche tags: modified_time: '2010-07-26T00:28:35.730-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6991263519641111952.post-539566130269854760 blogger_orig_url: http://blog.wizche.ch/2010/07/tisat1-hb9de-in-orbit.html --- Last Monday, specifically the  12 Jul at 03:52 UTC the spacecraft carrying TiSat1 (HB9DE) has been successfully launched! 

Since then a lot of HAMs has reported contacts with the cubesat's beacon.
This is a great success for the whole team of students and professors that work in this project.

You can find out more about the mission status and the collected data at this URL:
